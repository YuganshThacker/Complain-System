About Us

Welcome to the CHARUSAT Student Complaint System, your trusted platform for creating a cleaner, more efficient campus environment.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between students and the administration by providing a streamlined process to address issues effectively. Whether it's a maintenance problem, cleanliness concern, or general feedback, this system ensures your voice is heard and your concerns are addressed promptly.

Why Choose Us?

  • User-Friendly Platform: Submit complaints with ease through an intuitive interface.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Stay updated on the status of your complaints at every step.
  • Commitment to Excellence: Our dedicated team ensures a quick response and resolution to all concerns.
